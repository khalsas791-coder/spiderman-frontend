The Legend

About Spider-Man

From ordinary to extraordinary — the story that inspired millions.

🧬

The Origin

Peter Parker — a brilliant but bullied high school student — was bitten by a radioactive spider, granting him extraordinary abilities and the mantle of Spider-Man.

⚡

Super Powers

Wall-crawling, super strength, spider-sense, and his iconic web-shooters. Spider-Man moves through New York City with unmatched agility and reflexes.

🏙️

Guardian of NYC

Swinging between skyscrapers, Spider-Man protects the citizens of New York from criminals, supervillains, and every threat the city faces — day or night.

❤️

The Human Side

Behind the mask is a young man dealing with school, love, and loss. Peter's humanity is what makes Spider-Man the most relatable super hero of all time.
